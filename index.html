How To Use

Browsing

Click on "Home" and search for your location or a time period. You can also filter based on farmer's markets, community organizations, food farms, or gleaner's. Then click on the links that show up to see who's selling and what they're selling (for farmer's markets) or what food is offered for free (for community organizations, food farms, or gleaner's organizations).

Listing

Click "Submit Event," which should take you to another website. Then, fill out all the fields.

About Us

Granola Marketplace is an all-encompassing stop for farmers and farmers' market enthusiasts alike. With a hundreds-strong database of farmers' markets, food farms, and gleaning organizations across the United States, shoppers can easily locate the market nearest to them for fresh, organic produce, baked goods, and handmade artifacts. Granola Marketplace aims to improve accessibility of food overall and connect family farms with potential customers.

Our Team

Granola's Marketplace was born at HackMIT 2019. Our team consists of four people very passionate about coding, social good, and fresh food.

Stats

Family farms get much of their income from farmers' markets, and with a growing demand for organic food especially, a network for farms to humans is helpful. And for food-insecure US residents, having a database of nearby community organizations offering fresh food and resources can be very helpful.
